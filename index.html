About Luminara

Ranger Weather Intelligence System

Luminara is a futuristic ranger operations and weather monitoring platform designed to provide real-time environmental awareness, live surveillance, weather tracking, emergency reporting, and AI-assisted patrol support.

Built for modern explorers, rangers, responders, and environmental monitoring teams, the system combines live radar intelligence, atmospheric analysis, communication systems, and interactive mapping into one unified control center.
